ABOUT US

History

EkoTech is a technology service company founded in 2014 by Mia Johnson. The company started out as a computing hardware supplier to businesses of various types, and has since expanded to include enterprise technology consulting, data center management, and technology business continuity management. 

EkoTech has expanded its client base and now serves over 2,000 businesses based in 16 countries.

We pride ourselves on the following values:

  • Excellence
  • Punctuality
  • Attention to detail
  • Ethical business conduct
  • Sustainable business practices

Why use us

Our business has a wide range of experience in effective information technology deployment. We understand that our clients expect nothing but the highest quality for their technology needs, and over the years we have consistently delivered on this promise. 

Our watchword at EkoTech is “Excellence in Technology”, and this drives us in everything that we do for our clients. Our trusted team of seasoned experts are on hand to provide you with all the information and support you need for your technology deployments.

We operate our business in a sustainable way by ensuring we use only energy-efficient equipment in all our offices, in line with Goal 7 of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
