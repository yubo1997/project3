Policies

Financing policies

  1. Customer payments: The company will communicate details of payment to customers and receive payments only through its official accounts.
  2. Financial accounts: All accounts and financial instruments are authorized solely by the Board of Directors, who must approve any changes to these accounts.
  3. Audits: All company accounts are subject to periodic audits, according to the schedule approved by the Board of Directors. An internally appointed Audit Committee would work with a Board-approved external independent auditor, and duly submit a final audit report.


Delivery policy

  1. Equipment delivery is subject to a timeline of 7 working days for local procurements and 14 working days for international procurements. The company reserves the right to make changes to these timelines according to prevailing external conditions. Customers will be provided with regular status updates.


Return policy

  1. Equipment returns are expected no later than 10 business days after delivery.
